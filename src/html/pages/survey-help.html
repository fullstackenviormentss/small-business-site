Why We Collect Economic Data

The data collected from our economic surveys help businesses make informed decisions about expanding markets, creating jobs, introducing new products and services, and locating facilities.

Establish the Legitimacy of Your Survey

Our surveys are only conducted by mail or over the phone. To check if your survey is legitimate, you can reference our Business Help Site. Select the survey you received, and follow the instructions to verify your survey.

If you have further questions about legitimacy, please contact our National Processing Center for help.

Determine If Your Survey Is Mandatory

Some surveys are required by federal law, while others are not. Your survey should say whether or not it is voluntary or mandatory.

Protect Your Information

The Census does not publish any identifiable information about you or your business. We review and analyze our data extensively and take special precautions to avoid disclosing the sources.
