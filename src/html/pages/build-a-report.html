Build a report

A Census Business Builder (CBB) report keeps your data in one place so that you can use it anywhere.

About Reports

The report feature of CBB allows you to find data that you can implement in your research. After finding the data you want, you can create charts to use in presentations, applications, and more. You can also download and print a report in CSV, Excel, and PDF formats

Benefits of a Report

Create a data-driven business plan

If you’re working on your business plan, you’ll want to have data about your customers and competitors. A report is the easiest way to see all of the relevant data in one place.

Apply for a loan

Banks and other small business lenders want to know about a business’s profits and losses, projected finances, challenges, and more. A CBB report can serve as a benchmark to compare your data with similar businesses in your region.


Need help applying data to your loan application? Check out this Small Business Administration checklist for more information about applying for a loan.
