My Competitors

Our Data Can Give You a Leg Up by Helping You Understand the Competition

Use Data to Learn About Your Competition

You may be wondering, “Who are my competitors in business?” The Census Business Builder can easily help you find information about your type of business.

Discover business patterns in your area

Need to know more about the types of similar businesses in your area? How about businesses in your area that might complement yours? Our economic data can help you learn how to identify competitors by offering insights into how much competition you will have and what their revenue streams are like. Available information includes:

  • Number of businesses
    • Total number of potential customers in your area
  • Population per employer
    • How many people exist per each similar business
  • Average revenue
    • The average amount of money that each business earns

Become a competitive employer

Want to become a more-competitive employer in order to retain your best employees? Our data can help you determine the “optimal” number of employees you will need for your type of business and how much you should expect to have to pay them so they will stay. Our information includes:

  • Number of employees per establishment
    • The number of people that each business employs
  • Average payroll for each employee
    • How much you should expect to pay your employees
  • Average revenue per dollar of payroll
    • The amount of revenue that each employee earns for every dollar of payroll that you pay them

Other resources

We recommend checking out third-party sites, such as Google, Bing, or Yelp to search for specifics about businesses in your area. We are unable to release specific data, including the names or addresses of businesses, due to federal law.
