My Customers

Our data offers insights about your business’s most important asset: your customers.

Use data to learn about your customers

The Census Business Builder (CBB) makes it easy to find information about your customers in one place.

Learn more about your customers’ profiles

Want to know more about the backgrounds of your customers? Demographic data can provide insights into the makeup of your community. Our available information includes:

  • Total population
  • Age ranges
  • Race

Discover your customers’ socioeconomic backgrounds

Wondering about your customers’ social and economic backgrounds? Socioeconomic and housing data can help you learn more about your customers’ employment or household characteristics, including:

  • Education levels
  • Employment rates
  • Average travel time to work
  • Homeownership rate
  • Average household size
  • Median rent

How your customers spend money

Curious about what your customers are purchasing? Consumer spending data can help you understand the types of products and services your customers may be interested in consuming. We have data on a range of industries, including:

  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Apparel
  • Restaurants
  • Education
  • Entertainment
  • Food
  • Healthcare and medical services
  • Household services
  • Transportation
