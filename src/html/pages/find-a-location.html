Find a Location

Location, location, location. It’s one of the most important parts of your business.

Use data to determine the best location for your business

There are many factors to consider when looking for a business location. The Census Business Builder (CBB) is a one-stop resource to help you start your research.

Look for your customers

When considering a location, you’ll want to check that the nearby population fits your ideal customer profile. How much do people make in the city you’re considering? Do they spend money within your industry? Factors to consider include:

  • Average age
  • Medium income
  • Consumer spending

Discover our customer-related data and learn how to apply it to your business.

Find your competitors

Before you set up shop, find out more about similar businesses in your area. Consider the average revenue for your industry, and how much each business pays its employees. Using economic data, you’ll be able to determine if a certain location will fit your financial goals. Consider the following pieces of data:

  • Number of establishments
  • Population per employer
  • Average payroll
  • Average revenue

Learn more about your competitors so you can account for the competition.
