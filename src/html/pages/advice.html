Need help? We’ll help you find the answers you need.

Data Dissemination Specialists

Data Dissemination Specialists (DDS) are Census experts who know how to find, use, and apply our data. Based in regions across the United States, DDSs facilitate community workshops and one-on-one trainings.

For more information about help available in your community, contact a regional office. You can also submit a training request by emailing clmso.training@census.gov. Please allow for approximately two weeks for a response.

Small Business Administration

The Small Business Administration (SBA) offers business counseling, training, and mentoring at their District Offices, Regional Offices, Small Business Development Centers, and more. Find SBA resources near you.

The SBA also has online resources to help you with your business — including help with business plans, analysis, and government contracts.
