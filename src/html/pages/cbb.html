Using Census Business Builder

The Census Business Builder (CBB) is a one-stop resource for information about your customers, competition, and community. This guide will help you find the data you need.

Step one: Select your business type

Choose an industry category or type in an industry title, NAICS code, or keyword to find your business type.

Step two: Choose your location

Use CBB’s location tool to locate yourself or type in a town, city, zip code, county, or state. You can change your selected location at any point.


TIP: Some industry data is not available at the zipcode level to protect the privacy of our survey respondents. If you’d like to view this type of data, please use a city, county, or state. You can always change your location later.

Step three: Find your data

CBB provides two options for discovering data — a map and a report. You can use either to find data about your industry and location.

Learn more about the data related to your customers, your competition, or finding a location.

Option 1: Use the Map

This method creates a visual representation of your data.

Click on the “Go to Map” button to launch a map of your location

Option 2: Create a Report

This method allows you to view data in a printable report format. However, you cannot view data related to several zip codes at one time.

Click the “Create Report” button to load your report
